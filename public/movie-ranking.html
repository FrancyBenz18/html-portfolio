My top 5 movies of all time:

  1. The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

    It's majestic, epic, romantic and dramatic all at the same time, while having a huge cast with incredible acting, an incredible score and so much emotion, closing in the perfect way the best trilogy in recent movie history.

  2. The Shawshank Redemption

    Just experience it, an amazing story of drama, resilience and comeuppance, a must watch for everyone.

  3. Forrest Gump

    The special tale of a special dude just making his way through life. Feels guaranteed.
